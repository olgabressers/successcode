The short answer

The weight gain is neither random nor permanent. Three things in your chart lined up at once, and in 2026 they're at a doubled peak. But the peak has an expiry: the loss window opens in 2027.

Why you gained. By the chart your body is a "keeper" type — a dense, full build that by nature stores easily rather than shedding; plus a metabolism that runs slow (surplus clears with a delay). And separately: your health sector holds a "star of movement" — meaning movement is your personal medicine, while stillness works directly against the body.

Why it won't come off right now. From 2024 and across the whole decade a line of accumulation and comfort switched on, and 2026 is its doubled peak: more than any year of the period, the body is set to store and retain fluid. At the same time the "star of movement" sits away from the health sector this year — so your main lever for losing is not at full strength. Storing is maxed while the loss tool is muted — that's why 2026 produces gain, not loss.

When you can. Your window is 2027: the "star of movement" returns to the health sector (the body responds to effort again) and the 2026 doubling drops away. The next strong windows of the same type — 2031 and 2035.

How. The chart names exactly one lever — movement (that same "star of movement" in the health sector). There is no separate "diet by the chart," and I won't invent one. In 2026 movement holds the line (stops further gain); from 2027 the same movement begins to genuinely reduce.

How your weight works, by the chart — point by point
  • 1 A body that stores Innate

    At the base of your chart is a "treasury star": a body type that is dense and full, not model-thin, and by nature accumulates rather than sheds. This isn't a "problem" but a base setting of the body — you don't "break" it, you manage it. It means weight always responds to lifestyle more strongly than for the naturally-thin.

  • 2 Movement is your medicine Your lever

    Your health sector holds a "star of movement." Its meaning is direct: while you're active, the body holds its shape; the moment life turns sedentary, weight comes fast. This is your one genuinely working lever for weight. Beside it sits a "slow" metabolism marker: surplus clears with a delay, so patience and consistency matter more than bursts.

  • 3 2024–2033: the accumulation line, peak in 2026 Why it's hard now

    From 2024 a line of comfort and accumulation switched on for the whole decade — the body leans toward rest, coziness, fluid retention and puffiness. 2026 is its doubled peak: more than any year of the period, the body is set to store. That's why the same lifestyle produces more gain this year than in neighbouring years — it's not willpower, it's the phase of the chart.

  • 4 In 2026 the loss lever is muted Why it won't move

    The "star of movement" changes position every year. In 2025 and 2026 it sits away from the health sector — meaning your main tool for losing is not reinforced in these two years. Accumulation is maxed while the lever is muted — that's the structural reason losing is hard right now, even with effort.

  • 5 2027: the loss window opens When

    In 2027 the "star of movement" returns to the health sector — the body responds to effort again — and at the same time the 2026 accumulation doubling eases. It's the first year effort starts to show visible results. The same strong type of window repeats in 2031 and 2035.
