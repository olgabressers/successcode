A thousand years of precision

The system that read the lives of the Chinese emperors.

Zi Wei Dou Shu is the ancient Chinese destiny map, developed at the imperial court. For a thousand years the system remained closed to ordinary people — used only by emperors and their inner circle when making pivotal decisions of state and personal importance.

The reason for the strict secrecy was the extraordinary precision of the system. The map is calculated from the exact date, time and place of birth, and shows the structure of a life to the accuracy of a year and a month. Access to such an instrument meant access to power. That is why the system was kept in the imperial archives for centuries and passed only to a chosen few.

Today SuccessCode makes this instrument available to those who make responsible decisions and want to understand the structure of their own life.
