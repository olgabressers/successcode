The headline

In 2026 money comes not in small amounts each month but as one large sum at once. The year will not leave you without money — but it arrives as a single event, not a steady flow.

This is not a year of calm saving. Money comes in a jump: the sale of a property, a compensation, the settlement of a dispute, or a signed employment contract. Between those moments things are quiet — and that is normal.

And you will have to argue for that sum. The same money that arrives attracts other people's attention and calls for negotiation. So the task this year is double: receive it — and keep it.

Where the money will come from. By whichever route is open to you: the remainder of a compensation, the sale of a property, the settlement of a dispute, or a salary in a new job. The firmer you hold in negotiation, the larger the sum.

✓   Do in 2026
  • ●Bargain over any sum — this year money comes through an argument, not by itself
  • ●Look after money, documents and anything valuable — there is outside interest in them this year
  • ●Check every deal and every new person connected with your money
  • ●Keep the sum you receive, and plan spending from what is already in hand
  • ●Keep property ready to sell — a good time for a deal opens in early 2027
  • ●Put any agreement about money in writing
✗   Avoid in 2026
  • ●Do not expect steady income every month — the year is about one large event
  • ●Do not leave a large sum unattended — the outside interest lasts all year
  • ●Do not put money into "opportunities" that somebody else proposed
  • ●Do not accept the first figure named — this year bargaining adds money
  • ●Do not take on commitments against a sum that is not in your hands yet
  • ●A quiet month is not a failure: the money moments this year come in pinpoints
